About Jungkook

Get to know the Golden Maknae

Biography

Early Life

Jeon Jungkook was born on September 1, 1997, in Busan, South Korea. From a young age, he showed exceptional talent in singing and dancing. His passion for music led him to audition for various entertainment companies.

At the age of 13, Jungkook moved to Seoul to pursue his dream of becoming a singer. He joined Big Hit Entertainment (now HYBE) and became a trainee, working tirelessly to develop his skills.

Career with BTS

Jungkook debuted as the youngest member of BTS in 2013. Despite being the maknae (youngest), he quickly proved himself as a powerhouse performer with his exceptional vocal abilities and dance skills.

Throughout BTS's journey, Jungkook has contributed to numerous hit songs and has been instrumental in the group's global success. He is known for his dedication, perfectionism, and constant desire to improve.

Solo Activities

In addition to his work with BTS, Jungkook has pursued solo projects. His solo songs like "Euphoria," "My Time," and "Still With You" have received massive love from fans worldwide.

His debut solo album "GOLDEN" showcased his versatility as an artist and received critical acclaim. The album featured hit singles that topped charts globally.

Talents & Skills

Vocals: Main vocalist with a wide vocal range

Dance: Exceptional dancer with precise and powerful movements

Languages: Korean, English, Japanese

Other Skills: Video editing, photography, painting, sports

Achievements

Jungkook has received numerous awards and recognition throughout his career. He has broken multiple records on music charts and streaming platforms.

His influence extends beyond music, as he's known for his positive impact on fans and his charitable activities. Jungkook continues to inspire millions with his talent, hard work, and humble personality.
